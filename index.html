Stealth Ops

Stealth Ops is a card game where players create a randomly generated base as they sneak through, attempting to recover a briefcase with intel. Each spy wants the intel for themselves and their organization, but they may have to work together to get it in the first place. Like most secret facilities, there will be guards to avoid, air ducts to crawl through, tools to utilize, and many other challenges to face that will require quick thinking and adaptivity.
Role: Gameplay Design
Contributions: Core Mechanics, Items
Engine: N/A
